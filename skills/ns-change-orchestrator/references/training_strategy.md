# Training Strategy Guide

Choose the right training delivery method based on your audience and complexity:

## 1. Train-the-Trainer (TTT)
- **Best For:** Large organizations with distinct departments.
- **Process:** Consultants train "Super-Users" (Subject Matter Experts), who then train their own teams.
- **Benefit:** Build internal expertise and long-term ownership.

## 2. Role-Based Training
- **Best For:** Most mid-sized implementations.
- **Process:** Sessions are grouped by functional role (e.g., "Accounts Payable Training").
- **Benefit:** Highly relevant and efficient.

## 3. Hands-On Workshops
- **Best For:** Complex processes (e.g., Project Billing).
- **Process:** Users perform real tasks in a Sandbox environment with guidance.
- **Benefit:** Builds confidence and identifies edge cases.

## 4. Documentation & Videos
- **Best For:** Simple tasks or remote teams.
- **Process:** Self-paced guides and short screen recordings.
- **Benefit:** Scaleable and always available for new hires.
