# Stakeholder Interview Questionnaire

This questionnaire provides a starting point for Discovery sessions. Tailor these questions based on the **Project Origin** (Greenfield/Brownfield) and the specific stakeholder's role.

---

## 1. General Questions (All Roles)

### Strategic Context
- What are the top 3 pain points you hope NetSuite will solve?
- What does success look like for your department 6 months after go-live?
- Are there any specific reports or metrics you currently struggle to produce?

### Brownfield vs. Greenfield
- **[Brownfield]** Walk me through a typical transaction in your current system. Where does it get "stuck"?
- **[Brownfield]** What manual workarounds (Excel, paper) do you use because the current system is limited?
- **[Greenfield]** How do you currently manage these processes (e.g., in your head, basic spreadsheets)?
- **[Greenfield]** What are the "must-have" features you've seen in other ERPs or want to implement from day one?

---

## 2. Role-Specific Questions

### Executive Sponsor / CFO
- What is the primary business driver for this project?
- What are the key milestones or dates we must hit (e.g., year-end, board meeting)?
- What is your risk tolerance for go-live?

### Finance / Controller
- What is your current Chart of Accounts structure? Any planned changes?
- How do you handle multi-currency or multi-subsidiary consolidations today?
- Walk me through your month-end close process. How long does it take?

### AP / AR Manager
- How many invoices do you process per month?
- Do you have a formal approval workflow for bills/expenses?
- How do you handle customer credit limits and collections?

### Sales / CRM
- How are leads converted to orders today?
- What is your pricing/discounting logic? Is it complex?
- Do you pay commissions? How are they calculated?

### Operations / Inventory
- How do you track stock levels across multiple locations?
- What is your fulfillment process (Pick/Pack/Ship)?
- Do you handle returns (RMAs)? What is that process?

---

## 3. Technical Stakeholders (IT / SysAdmin)

- What systems need to be replaced or integrated?
- Who owns the data in those systems today?
- What is your preferred integration method (API, CSV, Middleware)?
- Are there any security or compliance requirements (SOX, GDPR, etc.)?
