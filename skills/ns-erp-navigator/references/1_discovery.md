# Phase 1: Discovery / Business Assessment

## Goal
Understand the business, scope the project, and define success criteria.

## Activities
1. **Stakeholder Interviews:** Meet with key department heads (Finance, Operations, Sales) to identify pain points.
2. **Current Process Review:** Document existing "As-Is" workflows.
3. **System Landscape Analysis:** Identify all legacy systems that will be replaced or integrated with NetSuite.
4. **Initial Scope Definition:** Define what is "In-Scope" and "Out-of-Scope" for the first release.
5. **High-level Solution Architecture:** Draft the initial conceptual model of the NetSuite environment.

## Deliverables Guidance
- **BRD:** Focus on "What" the system must do, not "How". Use `assets/BRD_Template.md`.
- **Roadmap:** Align implementation with business peak seasons (e.g., avoid go-live during year-end close).
