# Phase 7: Go-Live and Support

## Goal
Successfully transition from legacy systems to NetSuite Production and provide immediate post-launch support.

## Activities
1. **Production Prep:** Finalize NetSuite production account configuration (Company Info, Enable Features).
2. **Final Data Load:** Perform the final "Delta" migration of master data and open transactions.
3. **Cutover Execution:** Disable legacy systems and enable NetSuite for end-users.
4. **Hypercare Support:** Provide dedicated support desk for the first 2-4 weeks post-go-live.
5. **Project Wrap-up:** Conduct a post-mortem and transition to long-term support (FMA).

## Deliverables Guidance
- **Cutover Checklist:** Must be minute-by-minute for the weekend of launch. Use `assets/Cutover_Checklist.csv`.
- **Hypercare Log:** Track all post-go-live issues to identify training gaps vs. system bugs.
