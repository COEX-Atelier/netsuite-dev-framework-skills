### Phase 1: Discovery (CURRENT PHASE)
Our immediate next steps are focused on defining exactly what NetSuite needs to do for your business. We start with:

1.  **Stakeholder Interviews**: We need to meet with your Finance, Sales, and Operations leads to capture their specific requirements and pain points in QuickBooks.
2.  **Current State ("As-Is") Review**: Documenting how you currently manage your 3 subsidiaries and O2C/P2P processes to ensure no critical functionality is lost.
3.  **Data Migration Strategy**: Analyzing the historical data in QuickBooks to determine the cleansing requirements for a clean import into NetSuite.
4.  **BRD Development**: Drafting the Business Requirements Document (BRD) which will serve as our "Single Source of Truth" for the entire implementation.\n\n### Strategic Recommendations
- **Standardize First**: With 3 subsidiaries, use the Discovery phase to standardize your Chart of Accounts and approval workflows before we start building.
- **Data Cleansing**: Start reviewing your QuickBooks vendor and customer lists now; deduplicating this data early will save weeks during the Build phase.
- **Phased Training**: With 50 users, we will plan for a \"train-the-trainer\" approach followed by department-specific sessions in Month 5.
