# Project Charter
## Project Name: QuickBooks to NetSuite Migration
## Tier: 1
## Origin: Brownfield

---

### 1. Project Purpose
Replacing the legacy QuickBooks setup with NetSuite to support a multi-subsidiary structure (3 subsidiaries) and scale operations for 50 users. The project aims to consolidate financial reporting, automate Order-to-Cash (O2C) and Procure-to-Pay (P2P) processes, and ensure a clean migration of all historical data.

---

### 2. Measurable Objectives
- Consolidate financial reporting across 3 subsidiaries in real-time.
- Successfully migrate all historical data from QuickBooks with 100% reconciliation to legacy trial balances.
- Automate O2C and P2P workflows to reduce manual entry by at least 30%.
- Enable 50 users on NetSuite with role-based permissions and training completed.

---

### 3. High-Level Requirements
- **Multi-Subsidiary Setup:** Configure 3 subsidiaries with appropriate elimination and consolidation logic.
- **Order-to-Cash (O2C):** Sales orders, fulfillment, invoicing, and payments.
- **Procure-to-Pay (P2P):** Purchase requisitions, approvals, POs, receiving, and vendor payments.
- **Data Migration:** Import of customers, vendors, items, open AR/AP, and historical GL transactions.
- **Reporting:** Standard financial statements (P&L, Balance Sheet) by subsidiary and consolidated.

---

### 4. High-Level Risks
- **Data Quality Risk:** Historical data in QuickBooks may require significant cleansing before migration.
- **Process Complexity Risk:** 3 subsidiaries may have diverging processes that need standardization.
- **Resource Constraints:** 50 users need training; key finance staff may be overstretched during migration.
